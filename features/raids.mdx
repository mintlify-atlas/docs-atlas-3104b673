## Overview

Raid Battles are limited-time PvE events where you challenge powerful bosses with your team. Deal as much damage as possible to climb the leaderboard and earn exclusive rewards.

<Info>
Raids are turn-based battles where positioning, team composition, and boss mechanics all matter!
</Info>

## How Raid Battles Work

### Turn-Based Combat

Raid battles follow a strict turn order:

1. **Turn 0 (Pre-Battle)**: Your team's skills activate and stats are finalized
2. **Boss Phase**: Boss attacks and uses special abilities
3. **Player Phase**: Your team counter-attacks
4. **Repeat** until your team is defeated

<Warning>
The boss has **infinite HP** - your goal is to survive as long as possible and deal maximum damage!
</Warning>

### Key Mechanics

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="HP = Power" icon="heart">

  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Positioning Matters" icon="chess-board">

  </Card>

## Daily Tickets

Each player receives **3 raid tickets per day** that refresh at midnight UTC.

- Only your **best run** counts for the leaderboard
- Tickets are consumed when you start a battle
- Practice your strategy across multiple attempts

<Tip>
Try different team compositions with your 3 daily tickets to find the optimal strategy!
</Tip>

## Commands

### View Active Raid

```
!raid
```

Displays the current raid boss information including:
- Boss stats (HP, ATK)
- Your remaining tickets (X/3)
- Your personal best damage
- Top 3 leaderboard preview
- Event end time

### Start a Raid Battle

```
!raid_fight
```
or
```
!raidfight
```

Begins a raid attempt with your current team. The battle will:
- Display live turn-by-turn updates
- Show boss HP bar and damage dealt
- Provide interactive pagination to review the battle

<Warning>
Make sure you have a team set up using `!stb` before attempting a raid!
</Warning>

### View Leaderboard

```
!raid_lb
```
or
```
!raidlb
```

Displays the full raid leaderboard with:
- Damage dealt by each player
- Turns survived
- Paginated list (10 players per page)

### Raid Mechanics Guide

```
!raid_info
```

Shows a detailed explanation of raid mechanics including:
- Turn-based combat system
- Daily ticket system
- Strategy tips

## Strategy Tips

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build a Tank Wall">
    Place your highest HP SSR characters in slots 1-3 to absorb the most damage.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Leverage Skills">
    Pre-battle skills apply once before combat starts. Use buff/debuff skills strategically.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Watch Boss Patterns">
    Boss skills trigger on specific turn intervals. Plan your team to survive big hits.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Phase 2">
    At 50% HP, bosses become stronger. Ensure your team can survive the transition.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Battle Logs">
    Use the pagination buttons after battle to analyze what went wrong and optimize.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Battle Statistics

After each raid attempt, you'll see:

- **Total Damage Dealt**: Your final score for the leaderboard
- **Turns Survived**: How many turns your team lasted
- **Turn-by-Turn Logs**: Detailed breakdown of damage, skills, and casualties

<Note>
The battle viewer includes interactive buttons to replay each turn and analyze your performance.
</Note>

## Example Battle Flow

```yaml
Turn 0: Pre-Battle Skills
  • Warrior's Spirit: +10% ATK to all allies
  • Tank Stance: +20% DEF to frontline

Turn 1: Boss Phase
  • Boss attacks frontline for 8,000 damage
  • Hit Slot 1 Character for 8,000 damage (42,000 HP remaining)
  
Turn 1: Player Phase
  • Slot 1: 50,000 damage
  • Slot 2: 48,000 damage
  • Slot 3: 45,000 damage
  • Combined: 143,000 damage dealt

Turn 2: Boss Phase
  • Fractal Strike!
  • Hit Slot 1 for 8,000 damage
  • Shard forms (will detonate next turn for 3,200 AoE)

Turn 3: Boss Phase
  • Shard detonates!
  • All units take 3,200 damage
  • Slot 5 has fallen!
```

## Rewards

Raid battles offer:

- **Leaderboard Rankings**: Top players earn exclusive titles and rewards
- **Participation Rewards**: All players who deal damage receive end-of-event bonuses
- **Achievement Progress**: Raid participation counts toward achievements

<Tip>
Even if you can't reach #1, every attempt helps you refine your strategy and improve your score!
</Tip>

## Special Mechanics

### The Almighty Revival

Some characters have unique raid abilities:

- **Yhwach's The Almighty**: Revives the entire team once per battle (1 base revival + 1 per 5 dupes, max 3)
- Powerful for extending survival and maximizing damage output

### Debuff Tracking

Boss debuffs like Tessellation reduce your team's power temporarily:
- Duration counts down each turn
- Multiple debuffs can stack
- Shown in battle logs

## Admin Commands

<Warning>
These commands are owner-only and used to manage raid events.
</Warning>

### Start a Raid Event

```
!start_raid <boss_id> [days]
```

**Example:**
```
!start_raid SHYLEZSHKEBAN_ONYX 7
```

Creates a new raid event that lasts for 7 days.

**Available Boss IDs:**
- `SHYLEZSHKEBAN_ONYX` - The Onyx (5M HP, 8K ATK)

<Info>
When a new raid starts, all previous raid data is deactivated and the leaderboard resets.
</Info>
